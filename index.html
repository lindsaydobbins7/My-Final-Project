No Bake Bumpy Peanut Butter Nuggets

Ingredients

Directions

Prep Time: 15 minutes


Time: 1 h 15 m


Combine peanut butter, milk powder, and coconut in a large mixing bowl. Stir in oats, ground cinnamon, wheat germ, and apple juice concentrate until thoroughly combined. Shape the mixture into 1 inch balls. Chill thoroughly before serving; store remaining nuggets in the refrigerator

Bumpy No Bake Peanut Butter Nuggets
Ingredients




1/2 cup Peanut Butter

1/3 cup Rolled Oats

1/4 cup Unsweetened Flaked Coconut

1/4 cup Nonfat Dry Milk Powder

1/4 cup Wheat Germ
